Pandas are awesome!

The panda bear is a white and black, adorable animal that is native to China. Also known as the giant panda, this animal is known for eating bamboo.

Pandas are unique.

Panda diets consist of 99% bamboo. They can eat up to 40 pounds of it a day!
Most pandas are found in the forest-filled mountains of central China.
Wild pandas live up to 20 years and can weigh up to 200 pounds.
The giant panda is a vulnerable species and many conservation efforts are active.
